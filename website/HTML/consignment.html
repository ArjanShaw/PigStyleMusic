Consignment Program

Step 1: Bring records to the shop
  • Just bring your records during store hours
  • We may decline items that are unsellable (e.g., damaged, counterfeits, common singles)
  • You certify legal ownership and agree to keep your contact info current
Step 2: In‑store intake & barcode
  • Records should be reasonably clean
  • The store inventory system assigns a barcode for tracking
  • You’ll receive login details for the consignor dashboard to monitor your items
Step 3: Pricing & commission
  • You can set your own price, or leave the pricing to the store (based on Discogs / eBay)
  • Commission: 10% at ≤60% store capacity, scaling to 40% at 110% capacity
Step 4: Sales period (180 days)
  • 90 days at your price + 90 days with potential discounts
  • After 90 days: items may be discounted up to 50% (you choose to pull or accept)
  • Monitor sales real-time via portal
Step 5: Returns & pickup
  • Remove items anytime via portal (or let us know in store)
  • 30-day pickup deadline after removal
  • Unclaimed items become store property after 30 days
Liability & responsibilities
  • Store covers: missing items, theft, disasters
  • Consignor responsible: invisible defects (skips, etc.)
  • Affected records returned as unsold
  • Pick up unsold/returned items within 30‑day deadline
