Consignment Program

Consignor Dashboard: pigstyle.streamlit.app • Try the demo mode to explore features without an account.
Step 1: Portal Submission
  • Sign up and submit items via our portal
  • Portal checks for duplicates automatically
  • Store may reject items deemed unsellable (singles, counterfeits, etc.)
  • Must certify legal ownership and keep contact info current
Step 2: Pricing & Commission
  • Set your price or use the advised price based on discogs and ebay search results
  • Commission: 10% at ≤60% capacity, increasing to 40% at 110% capacity
  • Use your earnings as store credit and receive a 20% discount on store purchases
Step 3: In-Store Drop-off
  • Bring records to store
  • Records should be clean (cleaning service available at discount)
  • Barcode assigned for tracking
  • Items activated in your portal
Step 4: Sales Period (180 days)
  • 90 days at your price + 90 days with potential discounts
  • After 90 days: items may be discounted up to 50% (you choose to pull or accept)
  • Monitor sales real-time via portal
  • Receive email alerts
Step 5: Payouts & Returns
  • Request payout via portal when balance ≥ $10 (max once/month)
  • Remove items anytime via portal
  • 30-day pickup deadline after removal
  • Weekly email reminders
  • Unclaimed items become store property after 30 days
  • Use earnings as store credit to receive 20% discount on purchases
Liability & Responsibilities
  • Store covers: Missing items, visible damage missed at intake, theft, disasters
  • Consignor responsible: Invisible defects (skips, etc.)
  • Affected records returned as unsold
  • Pick up unsold/returned items within 30-day deadline
